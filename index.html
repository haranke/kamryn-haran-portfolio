About

Hello, I am Kamryn Haran. I am a junior at James Madison University, studying Media Arts and Design with a concentration in Interactive Design.

Ever since I was a child, I have always loved creating whether that was drawing, painting, or making YouTube videos. In high school I explored digital art and loved it, but I wanted to do more. I am very skilled in technology and thought why not combine the two which brought me to the world of interactive design!
SMAD has given me the knowledge and experience to design websites and apps. I hope you enjoy checking out some of the projects I have done during my career in SMAD at James Madison University.

App Design

I created an app called MyJourney with a classmate.

To make this app possible, my partner and I went through the design process of interviews, contextual inquiry, creating user journey maps, personas, and storyboards. Lastly, we created a low-fidelity and high-fidelity prototype which led to the final design.
